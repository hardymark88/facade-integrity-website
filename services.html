Services

Specialist support for facade condition, performance and remedial decision-making.

Facade Condition Surveys

What it is: Visual and technical assessment of glazed facade and curtain walling condition.

Typical client problem: Uncertainty over asset condition, maintenance liability or future repair budgets.

Report includes: Observations, photographic evidence, defect schedules, risk priorities and maintenance recommendations.

Suitable clients: Property managers, landlords, surveyors, asset managers and facilities managers.

Water Ingress Investigations

What it is: Structured diagnosis of leaks affecting glazed facades, curtain walling and envelope interfaces.

Typical client problem: Persistent internal water staining, tenant disruption or repeated unsuccessful repairs.

Report includes: Likely ingress routes, contributing defects, evidence trail, repair priorities and recommended next steps.

Suitable clients: Building owners, managing agents, insurers, occupiers and surveyors.

Curtain Wall & Glazing Diagnostics

What it is: Technical review of curtain wall, glazing, gasket, drainage, fixing and interface performance.

Typical client problem: System movement, failed seals, misting, impact damage, draughts or recurring facade faults.

Report includes: Diagnosis, system observations, potential failure mechanisms and practical remedial guidance.

Suitable clients: Commercial property teams, surveyors, facilities managers and project consultants.

Defect & Failure Investigations

What it is: Evidence-led investigation of facade defects, failures and performance issues.

Typical client problem: Need to understand cause, responsibility, urgency or appropriate repair scope.

Report includes: Findings, likely root causes, supporting imagery, risk commentary and recommended actions.

Suitable clients: Landlords, building surveyors, asset managers and legal or insurance teams requiring technical clarity.

Planned Maintenance Assessments

What it is: Assessment of facade maintenance requirements to support asset planning and budget forecasting.

Typical client problem: Ageing systems, unknown maintenance backlog or need for a planned works programme.

Report includes: Condition priorities, maintenance recommendations, indicative sequencing and risk-based planning advice.

Suitable clients: Portfolio owners, facilities teams, landlords and managing agents.

Remedial Consultancy

What it is: Development of repair strategies, remedial specifications and practical scopes of work.

Typical client problem: Need for a credible strategy before tendering, budgeting or instructing specialist contractors.

Report includes: Remedial options, recommended approach, specification notes, sequencing considerations and quality checkpoints.

Suitable clients: Property owners, asset managers, surveyors, project managers and facilities teams.
