Case Studies

Representative project scenarios for facade investigation and remedial advice.

Curtain Wall Water Ingress Investigation

Challenge: Repeated leaks affected office accommodation during wind-driven rain.

Investigation: Facade interfaces, drainage paths, gaskets and perimeter seals were reviewed.

Recommendation: Targeted remedial works to failed interfaces and drainage obstructions.

Outcome: Client received a prioritised repair scope suitable for contractor pricing.

Glazed Facade Condition Survey

Challenge: Asset manager required clarity on facade condition before lease events.

Investigation: Elevations were inspected for glazing, sealant, framing and movement issues.

Recommendation: Risk-based maintenance plan with immediate and medium-term priorities.

Outcome: Survey findings supported budgeting and informed landlord decision-making.

Remedial Strategy for Ageing Curtain Wall System

Challenge: Older curtain walling showed weathering, seal failures and variable repair history.

Investigation: Defects were mapped and assessed against access, disruption and lifecycle needs.

Recommendation: Phased remedial strategy with specification notes and quality controls.

Outcome: Client gained a practical route to extend service life and manage cost.
