About Facade Integrity

Practical facade consultancy from someone who understands how systems are designed, installed and repaired.

20+years designing and installing glazed facade systems

Led by Mark Hardy

Facade Integrity is led by Mark Hardy, who brings more than two decades of hands-on experience in the design and installation of glazed facade systems. That background gives the consultancy a practical understanding of how curtain walling, glazing, interfaces and envelope components perform once they are exposed to real buildings, real weather and real maintenance constraints.

The consultancy is independent, technically focused and commercially aware. Its work is not generic surveying dressed up as specialist advice. It is informed by the realities of fabrication, installation sequencing, tolerances, site workmanship, drainage paths, movement, access and remedial buildability.

Facade Integrity supports commercial property managers, building surveyors, asset managers, landlords and facilities teams who need clear evidence, credible diagnosis and practical next steps.

Independent

No contractor bias. No product agenda. Just evidence-led advice that helps clients make informed decisions.

Technical

Specialist knowledge of glazed facades, curtain walling, weathering, interfaces and common failure modes.

Practical

Reports and strategies that consider access, cost, disruption, procurement and long-term asset performance.
